## REDOX  
- **Reduction** – Loss of oxygen, gain of electrons, decrease in oxidation number  
- **Oxidation** – Gain of oxygen, loss of electrons, increase in oxidation number  
- **OILRIG** – Oxidation is loss, Reduction is gain
